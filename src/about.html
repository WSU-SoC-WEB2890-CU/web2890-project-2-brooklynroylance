H'Evans Honey is a family owned and operated company dedicated to providing quality honey to local communities at affordable prices.

Who We Are

We, the Evans, are just a small local family from the Northern part of Utah that fell in love with bees and honey. We thought there was no better living than to bee keeping and spreading the joy of honey to our community.

How We Got Our Name

Not long ago, before we started selling our delicious honey. A friend had their first spoonful of our golden goop and shouted, ‘That has got to be straight from heaven, it’s so good!’. From then on we called it H’Evans Honey.
